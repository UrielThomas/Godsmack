Godsmack itself is present in six countries: the United Kingdom, Russia, Switzerland, Belgium, Portugal and Spain. These subsidiaries target their respective national markets, taking into account specific local requirements and using the resources and global strategy of the brand. The international launch of Godsmack in 2015 represents an excellent opportunity for all these countries. This development, which has started with the marketing of ‘small transportable goods’ is the first step.
